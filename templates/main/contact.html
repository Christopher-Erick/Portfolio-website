Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about cybersecurity services and collaboration

What cybersecurity services do you offer?

I specialize in vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, security audits, SIEM implementation and monitoring, incident response planning, security automation scripting, and compliance assessments (ISO 27001, NIST frameworks).

What's your typical assessment timeline?

Assessment timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Network vulnerability scans typically take 1-2 weeks, comprehensive penetration tests 2-4 weeks, and full security audits 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline after reviewing your requirements.

Do you work with remote organizations?

Absolutely! I can conduct many security assessments remotely with proper VPN access and authorization. I'm experienced with remote security operations and use secure communication channels for all project collaboration.

What's your security assessment process?

I follow industry-standard methodologies including reconnaissance, scanning, vulnerability analysis, exploitation (if authorized), and comprehensive reporting with remediation recommendations. All activities are documented and performed within agreed scope.

Do you provide ongoing security monitoring?

Yes, I can help implement continuous monitoring solutions including SIEM configuration, log analysis automation, and regular security health checks. I also offer consultation on building internal security operations capabilities.

Are you available for full-time positions?

Yes, I'm actively seeking full-time cybersecurity analyst positions. I'm open to discussing opportunities in security operations, threat analysis, incident response, or vulnerability management roles with forward-thinking organizations.
